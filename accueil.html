About Us

A Mauritian law firm founded by former Senior State Attorney, combining government expertise with private practice excellence.

From Attorney General's Office to Strategic Private Practice

Precision Law Firm was founded by Mr. Jelend Chowrimootoo, Attorney-at-Law and former Senior State Attorney at the Attorney General's Office of Mauritius. His career spans both distinguished public service and private legal practice.

Known for his analytical precision, strategic thinking, and mastery of complex legal frameworks, he has built a reputation as a versatile practitioner capable of navigating demanding litigation, regulatory, and advisory matters with clarity and authority.

His tenure in public service strengthened his command of public law, statutory interpretation, and the procedural intricacies of the Mauritian legal system, positioning him as a trusted legal advisor on matters involving governance, administrative fairness, and the rule of law.

Commercial Law Contract Dispute

Complex Commercial Contract Interpretation

Successfully represented a major Mauritian corporation in a high-value contract dispute involving cross-border commercial agreements. The case centered on the interpretation of force majeure clauses in the context of international trade disruptions.

Key Legal Points:

  • Interpretation of contractual obligations under Mauritian contract law

Constitutional Division

Constitutional Law Judicial Review
Case No: CD 45/2022

Judicial Review of Administrative Decision

Acted for a group of applicants challenging administrative decisions affecting property rights. The case involved significant constitutional questions regarding procedural fairness and legitimate expectation in Mauritian administrative law.

Key Legal Points:

  • Application of Section 17 of the Constitution of Mauritius
  • Scope of judicial review in administrative matters
  • Protection of property rights under Mauritian law

Outcome: Successful application for judicial review, establishing important precedent for administrative procedure compliance.

2023

Industrial Court

Employment Law Workers' Rights
Case No: IC 89/2023

Unfair Dismissal under Workers' Rights Act 2019

Represented an employee in a landmark unfair dismissal case interpreting the new provisions of the Workers' Rights Act 2019. The case clarified employer obligations regarding termination procedures and severance calculations.

Key Legal Points:

  • Interpretation of Section 45 of Workers' Rights Act 2019
  • Procedural fairness in employment termination
  • Calculation of compensation under new statutory framework

Outcome: Successful claim resulting in significant compensation award and establishing important guidelines for termination procedures.

2022

Intermediate Court

Property Law Ownership Dispute
Case No: IC 67/2022

Land Ownership and Acquisitive Prescription

Complex property dispute involving competing claims to land ownership under the Affidavit of Prescription Act 2018. The case required detailed analysis of historical land use and statutory requirements for acquisitive prescription.

Key Legal Points:

  • Application of Affidavit of Prescription Act 2018
  • Evidence requirements for acquisitive prescription claims
  • Interpretation of historical land boundaries and use

Outcome: Successful defense of client's property rights, with court providing important clarification on evidentiary standards for prescription claims.
